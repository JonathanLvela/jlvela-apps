The approach

Built on a few simple beliefs.

Every app I build starts from the same place: what's the simplest, most private way to solve this problem well? No investor pressure, no ad revenue to chase, no growth-hacking. Just software that earns its place on your phone.

🔒
Your data belongs to you
All data stays on your device by default. No servers required to use core features.
💳
No subscription traps
Premium features are one-time purchases. You pay once and own it, including future updates.
🚫
No dark patterns
No fake urgency, no aggressive upsells, no notification spam. The app earns your trust.
🌍
Accessible to everyone
Core features are always free. Apps are localized for multiple languages.
